Who We are?

Inspire is an evolved & multifaceted Communication Design Studio. We began our operations in the Year 1999. For last two decades we are offering 'Signature Brand Solutions' in the areas of Brand Identities, Brand Promotion, Websites, Digital Marketing, Exhibition and Event Support, Space Designing and Facility Branding to our clients in varied industry segments. Our team is a combination of creative talents & business minds. Teamwork is the key word and every member of our team is sincerely dedicated for his skill set. Our head office is located in Pune and we have our presence in Mumbai with associate group company ‘Crosslink International’.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver highest level of customer satisfaction by blending newer strategies revolving around the core objectives. To grow with our clients and while doing so, ensuring our team meets challenges with zest and carries home loads of satisfaction.

Sanjay Kulkarni
Managing Director

Sanjay Kulkarni is the Managing Director of Inspire Group. An MBA in Advertising & Media, Sanjay has got wide experience in handling design and brand communication projects for more than 30 years. He has also worked as a Design & Brand Consultant for many national and international projects.

Sanjay is highly effective in collaborating closely with creative teams, marketing & brand managers, and design specialists to meet project needs with a proactive approach and task-oriented involvement. Sanjay's unique ability to intrinsically comprehend customer requirements and translate them into the most creative branding solutions has always given Inspire a cutting edge over its competitors.

Shama Moghe
Creative Director

Shama Moghe is the Creative Director of Inspire Group. Being a Creative Director for more than 20 years, Shama has acquired an insightful experience by working on diverse design and branding projects from a cross-section of industry segments.

Shama's ability to get into the depths of Brand communication ensures the fulfillment of achieving effective creative outputs with desired objectives for every project. Her eye for detail sets a quality mark for the efficient studio processes. She implements a proactive methodology to expedite projects and displays a unique ability to find graphic expression gaps to enhance design communication.

Dr. Nitin Parab
Brand Consultant & Strategic Adviser

Dr. Nitin Parab is a corporate warrior with over 20 years of experience.
